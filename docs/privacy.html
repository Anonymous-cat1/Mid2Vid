Privacy Policy for Mid2Vid

Last Updated: December 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Mid2Vid ("the Bot") collects, uses, and deletes data. As the sole developer ("I"), I am committed to transparency and data minimization.

1. Information I Collect

I collect the absolute minimum amount of data required to operate the service:

  • Uploaded Content: I temporarily process the MIDI files you upload solely to generate the requested audio/video.
  • User IDs: I store your unique Discord User ID (provided by Discord, not generated by the Bot) only in the following cases:
    • To save your specific bot preferences (e.g., disabling auto-generation or setting a default *SoundFont).
    • Temporarily while your file is in the processing queue (to manage concurrent limits).
  • Anonymous Usage Statistics: I collect aggregated, non-personally identifiable data, such as the total number of MIDIs generated, timestamps of activity, and the popularity of specific *SoundFonts. This data is not linked to your User ID.

2. How I Use Your Information

  • To convert your MIDI files into audio or video.
  • To enforce rate limits and prevent spam.
  • To analyze bot performance and *SoundFont popularity using anonymous statistics.

3. Data Retention (The "Zero-Storage" Policy)

I take your file security seriously:

  • MIDI Files & Generated Outputs: All files are processed on my private server and are deleted immediately after the conversion is sent to Discord. I do not archive your music.
  • Logs: I do not retain logs of user commands or chat history.

4. Discord Interactions & Message Deletion

To minimize local data storage, the Bot utilizes Discord's infrastructure to track what conversions came from you, and to host conversions.:

  • Mention-Based Logging: The Bot mentions you in the conversion response. This allows the Bot to use Discord's servers to associate the interaction with your User ID instead of maintaining a local database of your history.
  • Deletion Limitations: Requesting a "data deletion" from the Bot clears your locally stored *preferences*. It does not delete the conversion messages or history, as these reside on Discord's servers. To remove these conversions, you must delete the messages manually via the Bot (by reacting with ‚ùå on the message containing the conversion), or by contacting Discord Support.

5. Data Security

Your data is processed on a private server with restricted access. I implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of the temporary data processed by the Bot.

6. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If I become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, I will take steps to delete such information immediately.

7. Third-Party Sharing

I do not share, sell, or rent your data. All file processing occurs locally on my own private infrastructure.

8. Contact & Data Removal

To clear your saved preferences, you may use the bot's built-in reset commands. For other inquiries, specific data deletion requests, or privacy concerns, you can contact me via:

  • Discord: Join the Support Server
